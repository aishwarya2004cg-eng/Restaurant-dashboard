# Restaurant dashboard
A Power BI dashboard showcasing 50 global Michelin-star restaurants, featuring KPIs, filters, and visuals (map, charts, table) to compare restaurant ratings, pricing, star categories, and geographic distribution across continents

This is a Power BI business intelligence dashboard that visualizes performance data for top Michelin-starred restaurants worldwide. Here's a breakdown of its components:
KPI Summary Cards (Top Right)

23.22 — Average years in operation
14K — Average price in USD
4.90 — Maximum customer rating
50 — Total number of restaurants tracked

Filters / Slicers (Left Panel)
Interactive dropdowns for Price Category, Continent, Cuisine Type, Michelin Stars, and Country — allowing users to drill down into specific segments
Visualizations
Donut Chart — Restaurants by Star Category & Continent
Shows continental distribution: Europe leads with 25 restaurants, followed by North America (11), Asia (10), and South America (4)
Scatter Plot — Price
Plots Average Price (USD) against Sum of Michelin Stars by continent, revealing that European restaurants tend to cluster at higher price points
Line Chart — Restaurant Name by Rating & Star Category
Tracks the sum of average price across Michelin star levels (0–3), showing a sharp increase peaking at 7.6K for 3-star restaurants.
Map — Country and Restaurant Name
A geographical map color-coded by rating (4.5–4.9), showing restaurant locations across continents.
Data Table (Bottom Right)
Lists individual restaurants with columns for Continent, Restaurant Name, Average Price (USD), Michelin Stars, Average Rating, and Price Category — with Asia and Europe prominently represented.
Overall Purpose
The dashboard helps stakeholders compare restaurant prestige, pricing, geography, and ratings to identify trends in the fine dining industry globally
